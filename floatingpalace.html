● 26-Dec-21 07:24 PM
The Floating Palace, from a distance, is reminiscent of the oceanic garbage patches of a Low TL, socially backward world. Only this hodgepodge is the refuse of the aristocratic lactoderm from a dozen fallen worlds. Exceptionally ostentatious buildings, some made entirely of organically grown precious crystals, or graviton doped meta-materials that defy classical structural integrity or even rare stone but carved by long lost masonic talents that were once the envy of charted space - each branch outward with cancerous outgrowths, generations of diminishing talents trying desperately to build living space outwards and upwards. It's reminiscent of slime mould trying to escape depleted, nutrient-less, humus. On this date a classic starship, not seen for hundreds of years, reminiscent of a bird-of-prey, takes off quietly from the only Royal docking enclosure not given up to the rest of Palatial society to compensate for the loss of their main starport. (edited)
(edited)
The floating structure is currently ascending to clear the mountains south of Zusharay, a bone chilling bite in the misty air. A welcome respite from the tainted atmosphere lower down. The streets of the Palace, beyond the confines of the inner protectorate meant only for the increasingly inbred "clans of historical repute," are the descendants of aristocrats who have lost privileges or have been ousted in some political skullduggery. They intermingle with the often better skilled descendants of Palatial staff or outsiders who have somehow found themselves in such a place. A certain gaudy flamboyance infects all who live here. From gold laced epidermal augments to repaired classic vehicles that have no function beyond a high tech palanquin circling the paltry square milage of the crumbling grounds. Some who arrive, however, know of a more... lucrative side of the Palace. (edited)
